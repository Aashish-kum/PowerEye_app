PowerEye Monitor was designed and developed by a dedicated team of 4 members — combining frontend craft, backend power, IoT hardware expertise, and collaborative support to bring real-time grid monitoring to life.

The Project
What is PowerEye Monitor?

Mission

To provide electricity departments with a unified, real-time platform to monitor voltage levels across every pole in the grid — reducing outages, detecting faults instantly, and empowering field engineers with actionable data.

How It Works

IoT sensors attached to electricity poles transmit live voltage readings to our backend. The data is processed, stored, and visualised in a live dashboard — accessible securely by verified employees through email and employee ID login.

Core Goals

Minimise manual pole inspections, enable rapid fault response, provide historical voltage analytics for infrastructure planning, and secure sensitive grid data behind role-based employee access control.

Impact

From real-time fault alerts to automated shift reports, PowerEye Monitor turns raw voltage data into decisions — helping grid operators prevent blackouts before they happen and maintain safer, more reliable infrastructure.
